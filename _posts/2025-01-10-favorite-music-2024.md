## The List 💿

30\. **The Donkey - Couch Slut** - wild stuff, love the storytelling

29\. **Atom Bomb - Bully** - sad and sweet

28\. **Death & Romance - Magdalena Bay**

27\. **Changes Shapes - Lauren Mayberry** - that "yeah right"?? Come onnnnn

26\. **Starchild - Sweet Pill**

25\. **Not Like Us - Kendrick Lamar**

24\. **But Not Kiss - Faye Webster**

23\. **My Golden Years - The Lemon Twigs** - delightful fun rock and roll

22\. **Nothing Matters - The Last Dinner Party**

21\. **Sticky - Tyler, The Creator** - this goes hard

20\. **Take A Bite - beabadoobee**

19\. **Highlands - Middle Kids**

18\. **Jerkin' - Amyl and The Sniffers**

17\. **Sinner - The Last Dinner Party**

16\. **Coffee - Hinds**

15\. **Guess - Charli xx & Billie Eilish** - banger

14\. **Steal the Night - SKORTS** - best NYC band right now. See 'em live. This one soars.

13\. **AA  BOUQUET  FOR  YOUR  180  FACE - Saya Gray**

12\. **En Forma - Hinds**

11\. **Favourite - Fontaines D.C.** - gorgeous

10\. **Birds of a Feather - Billie Eilish** - instant classic

9\. **Bend by Middle Kids** - peak emotional power, big ballad vocals and that voice crack is irresistable. Chills.

8\. **Beaches by beabadoobee** - slick kinda throwback sound

7\. **Balloon - Tyler, The Creator & Doechii** - Doechii's airboat line when the music drops out is my favorite moment all year

6\. **Superstar - Hinds** - undeniable singalong anthem rock and roll thru and thru

5\. **DENIAL IS A RIVER - Doechii** - this is music. cannot stop listening. how is this song under 3 minutes??

4\. **The Feminine Urge - The Last Dinner Party** - vocal melody in choruses 10/10

3\. **Right Back to It - Waxahatchee** - perfect song, great harmonies, stunning lyrics

2\. **Good Luck, Babe - Chappell Roan** - the weird slow down key change plot twist at the end is 10/10

1\. **Starburster - Fontaines D.C.** - energy wayyy on a different level on this one. Seeing this live was fav concert moment of 2024.
